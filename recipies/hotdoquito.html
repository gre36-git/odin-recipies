Hotdoquito

    INGREDIENTS
  • 2 koroger hot dogs
  • 4 corn tortillas
  • salsa, ketchup, whatever sauce
  • shredded cheese
  • 1/2 oz citrus pickled onions
  • cooking oil
    DIRECTIONS
  1. Bing a skillet to medium high heat. Heat corn tortillas until soft and remove.
  2. Sprinkle (all the) cheese on the tortillas. Cut Hot dogs in half lengthwise and place one on each tortilla and roll them closed.
  3. Add oil to skillet and place tortillas in pan. Use cooking implements or hands to keep pressure on tortillas; rotating them as each side becomes browned.
  4. Once tortillas are cooked to desired burntness, remove from heat. Please turn off stove (remember last time what happened?).
  5. Pour sauce/salse of choice along "taquito" like objects and garnish with picked onions.
  6. Enjoy alone.

Pro-tip

Run room-temperature water over burnt hands. Pat dry and wrap with non-stick gauze.
