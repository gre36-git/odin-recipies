Hotdocket

    INGREDIENTS
  • 2 premium Kroger brand hot dogs
  • 1 hot pocket
  • crippling loneliness
    DIRECTIONS
  1. Remove hotpocket and microwave 1:30 seconds.
  2. Stare out kitchen window at 3 AM. Reflect on how and why no one will ever love you ever again. Return to reality as the microwave dings.
  3. Using a plastic knife, disembowl the hot pocket. Choice of eating it now or later (technically it's a side dish).
  4. Place two room temperature Kroger brand hot dogs inside empty (just like you) hot pocket roll. Microwave for 30.
  5. Serve and chase with shot of Pepto.
